Partners

GWI Tech is proud to partner with other organizations committed to furthering our mission to promote Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM), and to increase the visibility of female role models in STEM.

We are always looking for additional partners interested in offering paid or unpaid internships in established tech companies and well-funded startups. Additionally, we are always looking for organizations who are looking to sponsor our Silicon Valley Internship and Mentorship Programs, and other programming for GWI Tech.

Please contact us at info@gwitech.org if you are interested.
